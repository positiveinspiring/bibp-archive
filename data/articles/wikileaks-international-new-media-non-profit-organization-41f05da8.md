# WikiLeaks - International New Media Non-Profit Organization

> Source: https://www.bibliotecapleyades.net/SOCIOPOLITICA/sociopol_wikileaks.htm

WikiLeaks is an international new media non-profit organization
that publishes submissions of otherwise unavailable documents
from anonymous news sources and leaks.
WikiLeaks has won a number of awards, including the 2008 Economist magazine New Media Award.
In June 2009, WikiLeaks and Julian Assange won the Amnesty International Human Rights Reporting Award
(New Media).
